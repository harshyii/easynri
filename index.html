Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from Indian students

For Canada PR under Express Entry, we recommend aiming for:

  • CLB 9 (IELTS 7.0 in Listening, 7.0 in Speaking, 6.5 in Reading, 6.5 in Writing) for maximum CRS points
  • Minimum CLB 7 (IELTS 6.0 in all bands) to be eligible

Most of our Indian students achieve CLB 8-9 with our targeted preparation strategies.

Preparation time varies based on your current English level:

  • Basic English (10th pass level): 3-4 months intensive coaching
  • Intermediate English (12th pass level): 2-3 months focused training
  • Good English (college graduates): 4-6 weeks test strategies

We conduct a free assessment test to recommend the ideal preparation duration for you.

This depends on your strengths:

Factor IELTS PTE
Speaking Face-to-face with examiner Computer-based, less nervousness
Writing Handwritten, more flexible Typing speed matters
Results 13 days 2-5 days

Many Indian students find PTE easier if they're comfortable with computers. We offer a free trial test to help you decide.

The key documents we help you prepare include:

  • Valid passport
  • IELTS/PTE scorecard
  • Letter of Acceptance from Canadian institution
  • Proof of funds (minimum CAD 20,635 for 2025)
  • Academic documents (attested)
  • SOP (Statement of Purpose)
  • Medical exam report
  • GIC (Guaranteed Investment Certificate) - CAD 10,000

Our visa experts provide complete document checklist and verification service.
